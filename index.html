✨ Biodiversity Battle: Delhi vs Andaman & Nicobar & Lakshadweep ✨

Exploring Nature’s Purple Kingdoms 🟣🌿

🌆 Delhi

Delhi is primarily an urban area with limited natural biodiversity. It hosts some birds, reptiles, and urban plants in parks and riverbanks.

🌴 Andaman & Nicobar Islands

The Andaman & Nicobar Islands boast rich tropical rainforests, unique endemic species, and diverse marine life including coral reefs and dugongs.

🏝️ Lakshadweep

Lakshadweep consists of coral atolls with rich marine biodiversity, including corals, sea turtles, and various fish species.

🔚 Conclusion

Delhi has limited biodiversity due to urbanization, whereas Andaman & Nicobar Islands and Lakshadweep boast rich, diverse ecosystems. Conservation efforts are crucial, especially for the fragile island environments.
